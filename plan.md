## Design System

- Layout: 12-column grid, responsive mobile-first
- Components:
  - Buttons (primary, secondary, ghost)
  - Cards (project preview, testimonial)
  - Form fields (input, textarea, select)
  - Badge/Tag (skills, status)
- Interaction:
  - Hover states, focus rings, motion (0.2s ease)
  - Accessibility: contrast 4.5:1, keyboard nav, aria labels
